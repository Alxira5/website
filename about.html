My name is Guillermo Brito or also known as Alxira5, I am software developer and a digital artist, I am also a defender of free culture and that is why all my projects are free.

Since I was little, I have had a great interest in machines and fictional worlds, this is the reason why I have software development as a profession, since for me, creating software is like creating a work of art that will be in the hearts of some people. In addition to being a software developer, I am also a digital artist and I love making games and conceptual arts.

When I talk about defending free culture, I mean defending the possibility of sharing your works with others without the need to take away their freedoms, because even if you don't believe it, many of today's works use a free work as a basis and are not completely free.
